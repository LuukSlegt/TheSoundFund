How It Works

  1. You choose the tracks — you decide what to offer (only existing, streaming songs).

About The SoundFund

The SoundFund was founded by Luuk Slegt to solve a simple but urgent problem in the music world: independent artists were creating valuable work — but had no access to capital unless they gave up ownership, control, or both.

We exist to change that.

The SoundFund gives artists upfront funding by investing in the master rights to tracks that are already streaming. No pressure to recoup. No control over future work. Just fair, transparent support — built to help great music fund itself, sustainably and independently.

Our mission goes beyond just making deals — we’re here to rebalance the power between creators and capital, and to prove that fairness can scale.

FAQ

Do I lose control of my music?

No. You keep full creative control. We only take a share of the financial rights.

Do I still earn royalties?

Yes — you keep your share of the royalties, forever. We just receive the percentage we purchased.

Is this a loan?

Nope. This is a one-time investment. You don’t pay us back — we simply share in future streaming income.

Can I offer multiple tracks?

Yes. We can evaluate bundles or individual tracks. You stay in control of what you offer.

What happens if my track stops streaming?

That’s our risk, not yours. You keep the upfront cash either way.

What if I switch distributors?

That’s no problem. We can adapt to your setup — our agreement stays intact.
